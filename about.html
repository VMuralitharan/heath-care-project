About HealthCare

For over 20 years, we've been providing exceptional healthcare services to our community with compassion, innovation, and dedication to excellence.

Our Mission & Vision

At HealthCare, our mission is to provide accessible, high-quality healthcare services that improve the lives of our patients and the communities we serve. We believe in treating the whole person, not just the illness, with compassion and respect.

Our vision is to be the leading healthcare provider in the region, recognized for clinical excellence, innovative treatments, and outstanding patient experiences. We strive to create a healthier community through prevention, education, and comprehensive care.

Our Core Values

These fundamental principles guide everything we do at HealthCare and shape our culture of caring.

Compassion

We treat every patient with empathy, kindness, and respect, understanding that healthcare is deeply personal.

Excellence

We are committed to the highest standards of medical care, continuous improvement, and clinical innovation.

Teamwork

We collaborate across disciplines to provide comprehensive, coordinated care for our patients.

Integrity

We uphold the highest ethical standards, earning trust through honesty, transparency, and accountability.

Our Journey

From humble beginnings to becoming a regional healthcare leader, our story is one of growth and commitment to our community.

2002

Foundation

HealthCare was founded by Dr. Robert Smith with a small clinic in downtown Jaffna, focusing on primary care services for the local community.

2008

First Expansion

We opened our first specialty center, adding cardiology and pediatrics services to meet growing community needs.

2014

Hospital Opening

Our 100-bed main hospital facility opened, offering comprehensive inpatient and outpatient services with advanced technology.

2020

Pandemic Response

We led the regional COVID-19 response, establishing testing centers and vaccination clinics while maintaining all essential services.

2023

Telehealth Launch

Implemented our comprehensive telehealth platform, expanding access to care for rural and homebound patients.
